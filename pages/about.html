Sariah

Meet Sariah, she’s a typical 6-year-old sweetheart. To much of her parent’s dismay when asked what her favorite thing was she’ll tell you, “My Nintendo.”

However, when she doesn’t have her head down in a screen, she partakes a wide variety of activities.

She has a passion for all her animals. In her daily circle she adores on three different dogs Bear, Luna, and Ollie. There’s an outdoor cat named Bandit that she’s best friends with too. There’s even an aquarium in her grandpa’s computer room with 10 different fish and a bird too! Riah, our nickname for her, spends time with each of her little animals every day.

On top of her busy schedule taking care of her own animals, occasionally on her weekend outings she’ll traverse the greater metropolitan area visiting different petting zoos to help feed and love on all the local animals. Among all of them her favorites are the ponies, pigmy goats, and stingrays! Her favorite zoo is in St. Louis, Missouri and she most looks forward to seeing all the different bears.

Sariah is also a bit of a thrill seeker. Every trip to the amusement park she’s intrigued about her height so that she can see if she’s able to ride any new and scarier rides. She says that her favorite ride is the “chicken boat ride!” Which you ride a short canoe that can seat as much as four, it’ll send you on a Viking themed ride down a windy river with hills and drops.

Outside of that, her hobbies include dance, gymnastics, and ice skating. She very recently started school and told me that her favorite class so far was music.

Last and certainly not least of all from our young girl’s little perspective, but games, her favorite game is easily The Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild. On occasion she’ll request that I beat up Ganon; but she spends most of her time chasing and taming all the wild horses after rescuing them from evil goblins. She gets quite the kick out of beating me at Super Smash Bros with her favorite character, Zelda, of course. She is quite competitive when it comes to games!
